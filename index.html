Calm. Ambience. Dreams

Dreamemo is your personal dream journal where you can record daily entries about your dreams, feelings, and moods.

HIGHLIGHTS

Features you love

Dream journal

We provide a very intuitive interface for users to record their daily entries of their dreams into their personal dream journal. You can enter a descriptive narration of your dream, include your current feelings, and select your mood for the day.

Meditation

We also provide a feature users can use to relax and meditate. With soothing sounds of nature against the backdrop of a beautiful scenery, users can find solace and peace in different "environments," ranging from a cafe shop to a foresty jungle.

Inspiration

As soon as you log into the app, you will be greeted with a personal message based on the time of day, as well as a famous inspirational quote to motivate or brighten up your day. This feature has been discontinued due to logistical difficulties.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this product free of charge?

Yes, downloading the app does not require any payments from the user. However, premium features that include more variety of "environments," including new locations and sounds, do.

How do I toggle between different "environments"?

On the home page, you can click on the bottom center button that says "Change," and it will allow you to cycle through your "environments."

How do I save an entry into my dream journal?

Every 24 hours (starting 12:00 AM UTC), you can record and edit an entry into your dream journal. By clicking on the "Past" tab in the "Memo" modal, you can view all your past entries.

Will there be more features added?

As of now, progress on this project has been halted due to busy circumstances. If you are a developer who would like to contribute, please email me.
